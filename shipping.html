Shipping Policy

Fast and reliable delivery to your door

Shipping Information

We strive to get your adorable crochet toys to you as quickly and safely as possible.

Order Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1-3 business days to ensure timely delivery for our customers. During peak seasons or holidays, processing times may be slightly longer.

Shipping Options Available

Shipping Method Delivery Time Cost
Standard Shipping 5-7 business days $5.99
Expedited Shipping 2-3 business days $12.99
Express Shipping 1-2 business days $19.99
Free Shipping 5-7 business days FREE (Orders over $50)

Expedited options are available at checkout for faster delivery. Shipping times are estimates and may vary based on your location.

Transparent Shipping Policies

We provide clear shipping policies to ensure transparency and customer satisfaction with every order. You'll receive tracking information once your order ships, so you can monitor your package's journey to your home.

Shipping Destinations

We currently ship within the United States. International shipping options may be available in the future. Please contact us at contact@veloracraftstudio.store for international shipping inquiries.

Package Handling

All our crochet toys are carefully packaged to ensure they arrive in perfect condition. We use eco-friendly packaging materials whenever possible to minimize our environmental impact while protecting your precious items.

Important Notes

  • Shipping times are business days (Monday-Friday, excluding holidays)
  • We are not responsible for delays caused by weather, natural disasters, or carrier issues
  • Please ensure your shipping address is correct as we cannot modify addresses once orders are processed
  • PO Box addresses may require additional processing time
Questions About Shipping?

If you have any questions about our shipping policies or need assistance with tracking your order, please don't hesitate to contact us at contact@veloracraftstudio.store. We're here to help!
